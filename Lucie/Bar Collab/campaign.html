About the Bartenders

Steven Tseng is a mixologist and true artisan, turning his cocktails into an art form. With a string of accomplishments to his name, including being named the Regional Beverage Winner by Master of the Craft, Steven continues Mixology Class Instructor at W Toronto.


Brenton Mowforth is the owner and creator of CheersToHappyHour, an Instagram channel dedicated to exploring the art of craft cocktails. Brenton has been featured in numerous publications including Forbes and Liquor.com, does speaking engagements and live demonstrations, and is a judge for many cocktail competitions.


Lucie’s Raimey has spent over 20 years in hospitality, having worked across Ontario in various notable restaurants and bars. He previously was head instructor at the Toronto Institute of Bartending and studied winemaking in Niagara. He participates in competition bartending, and has made a guest appearance on Global Morning Live.


Lucie’s Sebastien began his career in France at a champagne bar before moving up to management positions in the East of France. Upon moving to Canada in 2015, he continued in mixology and opened Coq of the Walk (Absinthe Bar) and Kukum Kitchen alongside chef Joseph Shawana. Before moving to Lucie, he worked as a mixologist at The Commoner Room.
